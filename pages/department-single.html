Spine & Orthopedics

The Spine & Orthopedics department at VARDAAN Hospital Jind evaluates neck and back pain, disc disorders, spinal stenosis, and deformities—with a stepwise plan that prioritizes conservative care whenever possible.

When surgery is appropriate, our team discusses goals, risks, and recovery timelines clearly. Post-operative physiotherapy is coordinated to rebuild strength, flexibility, and safe movement.

What we offer

Clinical assessment with MRI correlation for disc and nerve-related symptoms.

Injection therapy, bracing, and targeted physiotherapy as alternatives or bridges to surgery.

Microdiscectomy, decompression, and fusion procedures when stable, lasting relief requires an operation.

Care highlights

Multidisciplinary follow-up ensures that pain control, wound healing, and mobility milestones are monitored after spine surgery or major trauma.

Sciatica and radiculopathy evaluation

Degenerative spine disease management

Post-operative spine rehabilitation protocols

Work and activity guidance during recovery

Second opinions for complex spine cases

24/7 trauma coordination when needed
